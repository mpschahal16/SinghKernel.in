About

The Asus ZenFone Max Pro M1 is a 5.99" phone with a 1080x1920p display. The Snapdragon 636 chipset is paired with 3/4/6GB of RAM and 32/64GB of storage. The main camera is 13+5MP for the 3/4GB model and 16+5MP for the 6GB model. The selfie camera is 8MP for the 3/4GB model and 16MP for the 6GB model. The battery has a 5000mAh capacity.
